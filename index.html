iPhone plus Apple Watch

Use each device for what it is good at.

iPhone is the full planner surface for editing, state changes, reorder, and longer review. Apple Watch is deliberately smaller: one-button capture and glanceable completion.

iPhone Plan, edit, move, and refine the daily log.
Watch Capture the moment and check off what is done.
Markdown Keep the same source of truth underneath both.

Private by default

Speech should not quietly leave the device.

On physical devices, Rivene requires on-device recognition for iPhone speech capture. If local recognition is unavailable, recording is disabled instead of silently falling back to server recognition.
