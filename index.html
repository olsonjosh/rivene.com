Capture the chaos. Keep the Markdown.

rivene is a fast daily log for iPhone and Apple Watch. Speak it, type it, check it off, move it, backlog it, cancel it, or leave it as a detail. The interface handles the tap dance. The file stays yours.

A planner that starts as a log.

Capture first. Sort later. Or do not sort at all, a shocking act of personal freedom. Today stays a real record of what happened.

Status without spreadsheet energy.

Done things stay visible. Moved things stay explainable. Canceled things stay dead. Details stay attached. The text underneath is ordinary Markdown, not a mystery vault with a support address.

Six states. Zero ceremony.

Long-press a row marker, pick what actually happened, and rivene rewrites the Markdown. That is the trick. Disturbingly useful.

Open

A thing still trying to become done. Brave. Annoying. Necessary.

- [ ] book dentist

Done

Finished, visible, and still part of the day instead of vanishing into a victory fog.

- [x] pay invoice

Moved

Not today. Not deleted. Future-you has been served.

- [>] prep screenshots

Backlog

Useful later. Obnoxious now. Into the holding pen.

- [<] compare export ideas

Canceled

Dead on purpose. A clean no with a strikethrough paper trail.

- [-] reply to mystery thread

Detail

Context, notes, links, ingredients, alibis. No checkbox cosplay required.
